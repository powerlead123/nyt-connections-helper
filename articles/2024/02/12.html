Strategy Guide

Today's puzzle features a nice mix of categories ranging from everyday objects to entertainment. Here's how to approach it:

1. Start with the Yellow Category

The candy brands category is the most straightforward. These are all popular chocolate bars you'd find in any convenience store. Look for well-known brand names.

2. Identify the Card Games

The green category groups together classic card games. If you see words related to playing cards or card games, they likely belong together.

3. Weather Words

The blue category contains common weather descriptors. These words often end in "-y" and describe atmospheric conditions.

4. One-Word Movie Titles

The purple category is the trickiest, featuring famous movies with single-word titles. These might be harder to spot as they could fit other categories at first glance.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Don't confuse HEARTS (the card game) with weather terms or emotions
  • MARS could be mistaken for the planet rather than the candy bar
  • UP might seem too simple for a movie title, but it's correct
  • BRIDGE could be mistaken for a physical structure

Additional Tips

  • Pay attention to capitalization - all words in this puzzle are capitalized
  • If you're stuck, try grouping words by their length or part of speech
  • Remember that categories can be quite specific (like one-word movie titles) or broader (like weather terms)
  • Don't be afraid to use your lifelines if needed
