About NYT Connections Game Helper

Welcome to the ultimate NYT Connections Game Helper! Our AI-powered assistant helps you solve the daily New York Times Connections puzzle with smart hints and interactive gameplay.

How to Play Connections

The Connections word puzzle challenges you to find groups of four words that share something in common. Each puzzle contains exactly 16 words that form 4 groups of 4 words each. Groups are color-coded by difficulty:

  • Green - Easiest category
  • Yellow - Easy category
  • Blue - Hard category
  • Purple - Hardest category

Tips for Solving Connections

  • Look for obvious categories first (colors, animals, etc.)
  • Consider multiple meanings of words
  • Think about wordplay and puns
  • Use our hint system when you're stuck
  • Remember that red herrings are common
