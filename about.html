How to Play NYT Connections

Game Objective

The goal of NYT Connections is to find four groups of four related words from a grid of 16 words. Each group has a specific theme or connection that ties the four words together.

Basic Rules

  1. Select four words that you believe share a connection
  2. Submit your selection to check if they form a valid group
  3. If correct, the group will be removed from the grid and its category revealed
  4. If incorrect, you'll lose one of your limited attempts
  5. Continue until you've found all four groups or run out of attempts

Difficulty Levels

The four categories in each puzzle typically follow a difficulty pattern:

  • Yellow: Easiest connection, usually straightforward
  • Green: Medium difficulty
  • Blue: More challenging connection
  • Purple: Hardest connection, often involving wordplay or obscure relationships

Tips for Success

  • Start by looking for the most obvious connections
  • Consider different ways words might be related (categories, synonyms, parts of phrases, etc.)
  • Use the process of elimination
  • Don't hesitate to use our AI hints if you get stuck!
